Frequently Asked Questions

How does Shoppoint App work?

You shop. You snap. We track, match, and reward.

After every purchase, snap your receipt in the app. We track your expenses, scan your items for brand and retailer deals, and give you cashback points when you're eligible.

You can also check prices, compare across stores, and plan smarter purchases to unlock rewards even before you spend.

📊 From spending to saving, we've got your back.

Is Shoppoint App really free to use?

Yes — you can start earning for free, no strings attached.
Snap receipts, earn points, and redeem them for airtime, data, and power. No fees required.

Want more? Join Shopper Club to unlock 3x rewards, exclusive missions, and premium cashback deals.

🚀 Free to start. Powerful when you upgrade.

What kind of purchases or receipts qualify?

Most everyday purchases are eligible. From groceries to fuel, restaurant meals to market runs as long as you have a valid, non-handwritten, readable paper receipt, you're good to go.

What can I do with my points?

Use your points to pay everyday bills no stress.
Redeem for airtime, data bundles, and electricity units from major providers.

You can even send points to friends or use them to unlock exclusive cashback boosters.

💡 Your receipts are literally helping you cover the cost of staying connected, powered, and online.

Is this legit? What about my data?

Yes — and we get it. You should ask.

We only use your receipts to help you earn points and track spending. We don't sell or misuse your info — ever.

🔐 If it's not safe for you, it's not good for us.
