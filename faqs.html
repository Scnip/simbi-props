FAQ

Find answers to common questions about Simbi Props, our UGC tools, and gypsum supplies.

🎥 UGC Tools & Equipment

What products does Simbi Props offer?

Simbi Props specializes in professional UGC (User-Generated Content) tools and supplies. We offer tripods, microphones, LED lights, and gypsum supplies. All our products are designed for content creators and businesses who need quality equipment.

Are your products suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our equipment is designed to be user-friendly for both beginners and professionals. Whether you're just starting your content creation journey or you're an experienced creator, we have products that suit your needs and budget.

Do you offer warranty on your products?

Yes, all our products come with a warranty. We stand behind the quality of our equipment and offer support to ensure you get the best experience with your purchase.

Is there a minimum amount to redeem?

Yes, the minimum redemption amount is typically ₦500. This ensures efficient processing of your cashback rewards.

� Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. We also offer express shipping options for faster delivery. Shipping times may vary based on your location.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within Nigeria. International shipping options may be available for bulk orders. Contact our sales team for more information.

Can I track my order?

Yes, you'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can use this to monitor your delivery status in real-time.

What if my order arrives damaged?

Contact our support team immediately with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or refund within 48 hours.

� Returns & Customization

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact our support team to initiate a return.

Do you offer customization services?

Yes, we offer customization for bulk orders. You can customize colors, branding, and packaging. Contact our sales team for details and pricing.

Can I order in bulk?

Absolutely! We offer special pricing for bulk orders. Reach out to our sales team at sales@simbiprops.com for bulk order inquiries and quotes.

How long does customization take?

Customization typically takes 7-14 business days depending on the complexity. We'll provide a timeline when you submit your customization request.

💳 Payment Methods

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money payments. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Is it safe to pay online?

Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption and PCI-DSS compliance to protect all payment information. Your data is never stored in plain text.

Do you offer payment plans?

For bulk orders, we can arrange custom payment terms. Contact our sales team to discuss payment plan options.

What if my payment fails?

If your payment fails, you'll receive an error message with the reason. Try again with a different payment method or contact support for assistance.

� Product Specifications & Support

What are the specifications of your tripods?

Our tripods are lightweight, adjustable, and compatible with most cameras and smartphones. They feature sturdy aluminum construction and can support up to 5kg. Check product pages for detailed specifications.

Are your microphones compatible with all devices?

Our microphones work with most devices including smartphones, laptops, and cameras. We provide adapters for different connection types. Check the product description for compatibility details.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team via email at support@simbiprops.com or through our website contact form. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Do you provide setup guides or tutorials?

Yes, each product comes with a detailed user manual. We also provide video tutorials on our website and YouTube channel to help you get started.

What should I do if a product is defective?

Contact our support team with photos or videos of the defect. We'll arrange a replacement or repair under warranty. Most defects are covered for 12 months.
