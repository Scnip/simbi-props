FAQ

Find answers to common questions about Shoppoint Rewards, cashback, and more.

💸 Cashback & Rewards

How do I earn cashback?

You earn cashback every time you make a purchase at participating stores or online. Simply use the Shoppoint app to check available rewards before you shop, and earn cashback automatically on your purchases.

What's the maximum cashback I can earn?

There's no maximum limit on cashback earnings. The more you shop at participating merchants, the more cashback you can accumulate. Different merchants offer different cashback percentages.

How do I redeem my cashback?

You can redeem your cashback directly from the app. Go to your rewards section, select the amount you want to redeem, and choose your preferred redemption method (bank transfer, mobile money, etc.).

Is there a minimum amount to redeem?

Yes, the minimum redemption amount is typically ₦500. This ensures efficient processing of your cashback rewards.

📱 Using the App

How do I download the Shoppoint app?

You can download the Shoppoint app from the Google Play Store (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS). Search for "Shoppoint Rewards" and install the official app.

What devices are supported?

Shoppoint is available on iOS (iPhone) and Android devices. You need at least iOS 12 or Android 6.0 to run the app smoothly.

How do I create an account?

Download the app, tap "Sign Up," enter your email or phone number, create a password, and verify your account. You'll be ready to start earning cashback in minutes.

Can I use Shoppoint on multiple devices?

Yes, you can log into your Shoppoint account on multiple devices. Your cashback balance and rewards will sync across all devices.

🔐 Privacy & Security

Is my personal data safe with Shoppoint?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and financial information. Your data is never shared with third parties without your consent.

How do you protect my payment information?

We use PCI-DSS compliant systems and end-to-end encryption for all payment transactions. Your payment details are tokenized and never stored in plain text.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can request account deletion from the app settings. Your data will be securely deleted within 30 days, though any unredeemed cashback will be forfeited.

What should I do if I suspect fraudulent activity?

Contact our support team immediately through the app or email support@shoppoint.com. We'll investigate and take appropriate action to secure your account.

🧩 Missions & Bonuses

What are Shoppoint missions?

Missions are special tasks or challenges that give you bonus cashback when completed. They might include shopping at specific stores, making purchases above a certain amount, or trying new merchants.

How do I complete a mission?

Check the Missions section in the app to see available tasks. Follow the instructions, complete the required actions, and your bonus cashback will be credited automatically once verified.

Do missions expire?

Yes, each mission has an expiration date shown in the app. Make sure to complete missions before they expire to earn the bonus cashback.

Can I get referral bonuses?

Yes! Refer friends to Shoppoint and earn bonus cashback when they sign up and make their first purchase. Check the Referral section for your unique referral code.

💼 Account & Support

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password," enter your email or phone number, and follow the instructions sent to you. You'll be able to create a new password.

How do I update my profile information?

Go to Settings > Profile, and you can update your name, email, phone number, and other personal information. Changes take effect immediately.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team through the app (Help > Contact Support), email us at support@shoppoint.com, or call our hotline. We're available 24/7 to help.

What should I do if I have a billing issue?

Contact our support team with details of the issue. We'll investigate and resolve billing problems within 48 hours. Keep screenshots of any transactions for reference.

Is there a FAQ for merchants?

Yes, merchants can visit our business portal at business.shoppoint.com for merchant-specific FAQs, integration guides, and support resources.
