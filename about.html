Professional UGC Tools & Supplies

At Simbi Props, we're dedicated to empowering content creators with the tools they need to produce high-quality user-generated content (UGC).

We supply professional-grade equipment including tripods, microphones, and LED lights that help creators capture stunning visuals and crystal-clear audio for their content.

Beyond UGC tools, we also provide gypsum supplies for businesses and professionals who need quality materials for their projects.

Our mission is to equip creators and businesses for success

We believe that quality content creation shouldn't be limited by access to professional equipment. That's why Simbi Props exists.

Whether you're a content creator looking to level up your UGC game or a business needing reliable gypsum supplies, we're here to provide affordable, high-quality solutions that help you succeed.

Our product range includes everything from professional lighting and audio equipment to construction materials, all designed to meet the needs of modern creators and businesses.
